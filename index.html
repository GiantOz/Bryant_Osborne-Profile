How I became a Web Developer!

My Name is Bryant Osborne, Thanks for reading this. After high School I joined the US Army and received an Honorable discharge. After which, I worked for the College of Pharmacy and Science then The City of Philadelphia until I decided to leave and start my own Business, which I still own today. While doing so I attended Community College of Philadelphia, The University of Phoenix and now UPenn. I just graduated from UPenn's Coding Bootcamp and Operate Top Flight Facility Maintenance which I have been doing since 2004. Next, I want to be a full time Web Developer, even though I will continue to manage my business I will work as a Developer in order to enhance my family life.
